Bio

Jia Lee is an illustrator based in New York with a BFA in Illustration from the School of Visual Arts. She was born in South Korea and lived in California for most of her life, and attempts to visualize her personal experiences in her paintings using broad themes of desire, identity, and power. Her main influences include Inka Essenhigh and Lisa Yuskavage.
